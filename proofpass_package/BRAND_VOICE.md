# ProofPass – Brand Voice

## Tone
- Confident
- Clear
- Technical but human
- No hype

## Messaging Principles
- Explain simply
- Avoid buzzwords
- Show, don’t tell

## Core Narrative
“Prove the fact, not the data.”

## Taglines
- Verify anything. Reveal nothing.
- Privacy-first verification
- Trust without exposure

## Do
- Use real-world examples
- Emphasize privacy benefit
- Keep sentences short

## Don’t
- Don’t say “zero-knowledge” unless real
- Don’t over-explain crypto
- Don’t sound academic

## Example Copy

### Good
“Prove you're over 18 without sharing your birthdate.”

### Bad
“Utilizing advanced cryptographic primitives to ensure privacy-preserving attestations.”

## Pitch Line
“This is the smallest usable version of private credential verification.”
